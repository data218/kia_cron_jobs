🧮 Dashboard Calculations & Logic

MetricLogic
New (LY)VINs with policytype="New" in the LY month.
New (CY)VINs with policytype="New" in the CY month.
Renewal (LY)VINs present in both the LY month and the same LY-1 month (VIN overlap).
Renewal (CY)A VIN is classified as CY Renewal if it had policytype="New" in LY months M, M-1, or M-2 (same month + 2 months back, wrapping to Nov/Dec of LY year for Jan/Feb) AND has policytype="Renewal" in the CY month.
Rollover (LY)LY VINs with policytype="Renewal" that are not in the same LY-1 month (= previously-renewed policies).
Rollover (CY)Remaining CY VINs with policytype="Renewal" that are not CY Renewal and not LY Rollover VINs that came back. Formula: Total CY Renewal-type − CY Renewal − LY Rollover in CY.
Rollover→RenewLY Rollover VINs that came to CY in the same month. Shows how many previously-renewed policies returned.
Not VisitedLY VINs that do not appear in any CY month within the selected range.
LY #Count of unique VINs in the Previous Year (LY = CY − 1) for the given month.
CY #Count of unique VINs in the Current Year for the given month.
Growth((CY − LY) / LY) × 100. Shows ▲ for positive, ▼ for negative, ◆ for flat. For Renewal, the base is LY New (not LY Renewal) since New policies are the pool that become Renewal.
Unique VINs (Annual)Each VIN is counted only once per year, regardless of how many months it appears. Info bar shows the true unique count.
Sum of Months vs UniqueMonthly columns count unique VINs per month. A VIN in Jan AND Feb gets counted in both. The total row shows the correct unique count across all months.
Duplicate Policy CheckDetects rows where the same policy number appears more than once in the same year.
Gross Premium (₹L)Sum of grosspremium for all rows, divided by 100,000.
Avg Premiumgrosspremium / policy count for the selected period.
Premium Share %Each company's gross premium as a percentage of total.
Cancelled / DeadPolicies where cancelled=Yes are excluded from all dashboard calculations. View them in Export Data → Cancelled.
